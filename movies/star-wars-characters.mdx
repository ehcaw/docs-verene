The Star Wars universe features a vast array of memorable characters spanning multiple generations and storylines.

### Other notable Jedi

- **Mace Windu** - Jedi Council member known for his purple lightsaber
- **Ahsoka Tano** - Anakin's former Padawan
- **Rey** - Protagonist of the sequel trilogy

## Sith and dark side users

- **Darth Vader** - Former Jedi turned Sith Lord
- **Emperor Palpatine** - The sinister Sith mastermind
- **Darth Maul** - Sith assassin with a double-bladed lightsaber
- **Kylo Ren** - Han and Leia's son who turns to the dark side

## Droids

<Info>
Droids play essential roles throughout Star Wars, often providing comic relief and crucial assistance.
</Info>

- **R2-D2** - Resourceful astromech droid
- **C-3PO** - Protocol droid fluent in over six million forms of communication
- **BB-8** - Poe Dameron's loyal astromech
- **K-2SO** - Reprogrammed Imperial security droid

## Bounty hunters

- **Boba Fett** - The galaxy's most notorious bounty hunter
- **Din Djarin** - The Mandalorian protagonist
- **Jango Fett** - Template for the clone army
