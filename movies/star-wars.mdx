# Star Wars

**Star Wars** is an American epic space opera multimedia franchise created by George Lucas. The franchise began with the eponymous 1977 film and quickly became a worldwide pop culture phenomenon.

## Overview

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Created by** | George Lucas |
| **First film** | May 25, 1977 |
| **Studio** | Lucasfilm (now owned by Disney) |
| **Total films** | 12 theatrical releases |
| **Academy Awards** | 10 wins |

### Original trilogy

1. *A New Hope* (1977)
2. *The Empire Strikes Back* (1980)
3. *Return of the Jedi* (1983)

### Prequel trilogy

1. *The Phantom Menace* (1999)
2. *Attack of the Clones* (2002)
3. *Revenge of the Sith* (2005)

### Sequel trilogy

1. *The Force Awakens* (2015)
2. *The Last Jedi* (2017)
3. *The Rise of Skywalker* (2019)

## Main characters

- **Luke Skywalker** - The hero of the original trilogy who becomes a Jedi Knight
- **Darth Vader** - The iconic villain, formerly Anakin Skywalker
- **Princess Leia** - Rebel leader and Luke's twin sister
- **Han Solo** - Smuggler turned Rebel hero
- **Obi-Wan Kenobi** - Jedi Master who trains both Anakin and Luke
- **Yoda** - Ancient and powerful Jedi Grand Master

## The Force

The Force is an energy field created by all living things that binds the galaxy together. Force-sensitive individuals can learn to harness its power.

<Info>
"May the Force be with you" has become one of the most quoted phrases in cinema history.
</Info>

## Cultural impact

Star Wars revolutionized filmmaking with groundbreaking visual effects and pioneered modern franchise merchandising. It has influenced countless films, TV shows, books, and video games.

The franchise includes:

- Theatrical and streaming films
- Animated series (The Clone Wars, Rebels, The Bad Batch)
- Live-action series (The Mandalorian, Andor, Obi-Wan Kenobi)
- Novels, comics, and video games
- Theme park attractions

## Legacy

> "A long time ago in a galaxy far, far away...."

Star Wars continues to captivate audiences nearly five decades after its debut, expanding its universe through new stories while honoring the themes of hope, redemption, and the eternal struggle between good and evil.
